Yunchu Zhang

I'm a Master’s degree student in the department of Mechanical Engineering in UCLA. My research interests include robotics and artificial intelligence. In the future, I look forward to pursuing a doctoral degree in robotics. In the past, I have done a lot of projects about robots such as intelligent robot camera with deep learning algorithm, the smart car project based on computer vision, control theory and embedded programming. I also worked for a quadcopter project about tracking object through vision. I used L-K pyramid on Opencv library for tracking and adopted the P- N method to make online learning for the robot, which could make it have a better adaptability for various object than off-line neural net training. Currently, I'm working in Doctor TC’s lab and planning to publish a paper in robotics area.

In addition to my interest in robotics, I'm also a young people who love culture and arts. I enjoy traveling and believes it to be instrumental to mental and physical regeneration, as well as learning about the ways of the world.
